7. CONTROLS FOR DO-NOT-TRACK FEATURES

Most web browsers and some mobile operating systems and mobile applications include a Do-Not-Track ("DNT") feature or setting you can activate to signal your privacy preference not to have data about your online Browse activities monitored and collected. At this stage, no uniform technology standard for recognizing and implementing DNT signals has been finalized. As such, we do not currently respond to DNT browser signals or any other mechanism that automatically communicates your choice not to be tracked online. If a standard for online tracking is adopted that we must follow in the future, we will inform you about that practice in a revised version of this Privacy Notice.

8. EMPLOYEE LOCATION TRACKING

Our app collects real-time location data of field employees during work hours to support essential business functions. This includes:

  • Verifying task completion
  • Confirming visits to assigned sites
  • Measuring distance traveled
  • Ensuring compliance with planned routes

To enable these features, the app requires background location access, even when it is not actively in use. This allows continuous location tracking to ensure operational accuracy and accountability. In future updates, we may introduce a map-based view showing historical location trails to further enhance transparency and efficiency. Location data is used solely for the purposes described above and is not shared with unauthorized third parties. Users can manage their location permissions through their device settings, but disabling background location access will impair key functionality of the app.
